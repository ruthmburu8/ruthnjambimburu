Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our farm, products, or services? We've answered some of the most common ones to help you understand us better.

Where is Ecogrow Farm located? +
We are located in Nairobi, Kenya, and serve both local and regional customers.
Are your products organic? +
Yes! All our produce is grown organically without synthetic chemicals.
Do you offer home delivery? +
Absolutely. We provide free delivery within Nairobi and surrounding areas.
Can I visit the farm? +
Yes! We welcome farm visits by appointment. Please contact us to schedule your visit.
How can I place an order? +
You can order via our website, phone call or by visiting our local retail parteners.
Do you offer bulk supply for businesses? +
Absolutely! We work with hotels, restaurants and supermarkets for bulk delivery. Get in touch to discuss your needs.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept M-pesa,credit/debit cards and bank transfers for all orders.
